Our site aims to bring greater awareness to mental health and become a one-stop shop for resources.

Y.U. Matter


Globally, more than 70% of young people and adults who suffer with mental illness do not receive any mental health care.

In the United States, there has been a stark rise in the mental health crisis. Inadequate access to mental health resources and continued stigma make it difficult for youth to receive the necessary support.

There are many resources all over the web. So we made a place where you can find them all in one place. They can be found in Resources.
